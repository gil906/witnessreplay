Welcome to WitnessReplay

We're here to help you report an incident — whether it's an accident, crime, or any event you witnessed. You can speak or type, and we'll guide you through the process.

🎤

Speak or Type

Describe what you saw in your own words. Click the microphone or press Space to start recording, or simply type in the chat. We'll listen carefully and begin building a record of the incident.

💬

Guided Reporting

We'll ask clarifying questions to capture the full picture. Answer naturally. If something needs correcting, just say so — "The car was blue, not red" — and we'll update the record immediately.

✅

Ready to Report

Your report is saved automatically. You can review the timeline, scene reconstruction, export to PDF, or start a new report anytime. Let's document what you witnessed.
